About the Department of MBA

Ever since its inception in the year 2007 Department of MBA has been flamboyant in all its endeavours. We have been bench-marking our moves with the coveted and leading institutions. The robust industrial growth and conducive business environment has hastened the pace of the Indian economy. This has opened new vistas to educational service providers to offer industry integrated education to cater of the needs to competitive business. Department of MBA, a Management institute with its own calibre is taking new and successful strides by offering quality and integrity oriented management education to its budding managers. The Management Department offers Two Years Full Time MBA Program, which is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, Permanently Affiliated and Autonomous under Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi. The programme is also accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi as well as it got A+ Grade by National Assessment & Accreditation Council (NAAC). Moreover, the Department of MBA has its own in-house Research Centre which not only offers Ph.D program through Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi but also undertakes and conducts various research projects on contemporary and relevant issues in the areas of management and technology. The department has strong and mutually beneficial connects with industry.

Vision

To be Recognized as a Progressive Department for Business Education, Research and create Meaningful Students Engagement for Learning Management Education.

Mission

Developing excellence through learning to develop effective and responsible leaders by creating insightful knowledge and inspiring students to develop managerial skills, problem solving ability, analytical and innovative mindset

Pedagogy

We provide management education on par with the best Indian B-Schools. We use prominent pedagogy tools like case study method, live projects and simulations to provide valuable insights of the industry. We strive to expand the knowledge horizons of the learners using tools like Corporate News Analysis, Group discussions, Management Games, Guest lectures and Library assignments. We also enhance the managerial skills of the students with an E-Learning facility in our advanced digital library.
